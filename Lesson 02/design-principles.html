Principles of Design

PARC: Proximity

Boca Juniors

https://www.bocajuniors.com.ar/

The principle of proximity is applied in the grouping of the articles that are offered to read and how they are related to each other. Colors are used a lot, especially blue and yellow, which are the colors of the club.

PARC: Alignment

Disney +

https://www.disneyplus.com/

In each title they are aligned to order the content. This helps you follow a line and know that the content is accurate and clear. This is reinforced with categories to differentiate the different genres of movies and series.

PARC: Contrast

Samsung

https://samsung.com

On this page the contrast is given in the logo with the color black. As well as throughout the page these two colors predominate (black and white), offering a strong visual memory to be able to relate the brand with these colors.
